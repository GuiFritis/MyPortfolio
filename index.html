About me

Hi, I’m Guilherme Fritis!

I’m a Game Programmer from Brazil.

I’ve had the experience of developing several games on my own, taking charge of every stage of these projects.

My strengths are in 2D games using Unity, though I’ve also developed 3D and have experience with Unreal Engine.

I am graduated in Development and Analyses of Systems, and I've worked for some years with Full Stack Development.

I'm constantly taking courses to improve my knowledge in game development. Making games gives me a great sense of accomplishment and joy, especially knowing that people can enjoy the experiences I create. That’s why I love it.

In addition to programming, I compose music in my free time. I have a passion for art and enjoy creating it. I've even managed to combine my love for games and music in a mobile project, though it hasn’t been published yet.

I’m also a scout leader, working with kids most Saturdays. Through scouting, I experience a variety of adventures and meet diverse people.

Last but not least, I’m an Pathfinder player in a campaign on weekends.
